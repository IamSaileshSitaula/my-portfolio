Vehicular Wind-Powered Vertical Axis Wind Turbine

Sustainable Energy Generation Research

This project involved the design, fabrication, and testing of a vertical axis wind turbine specifically engineered to capture and harness energy from vehicle-induced wind on highways, creating a sustainable energy source from previously wasted wind energy.

Key Contributions:

  • Designed an innovative turbine blade configuration optimized for low-speed, turbulent wind conditions typical of highway environments
  • Conducted aerodynamic analysis using ANSYS simulation software to maximize energy capture efficiency
  • Fabricated a working prototype using sustainable materials to reduce environmental impact
  • Implemented sensors to measure power output under various wind conditions
  • Tested the prototype in real-world conditions, collecting performance data that validated the concept

Outcomes:

  • Successfully demonstrated power generation from vehicle-induced wind
  • Achieved a 15% higher efficiency than conventional vertical axis turbines in turbulent wind conditions
  • Identified optimal placement parameters for highway implementation
  • Developed a scalable design that could be implemented along transportation corridors

Quality Control in Lokta Paper Production

Computer Vision Application in Traditional Crafts

This project focused on developing an automated quality control system using computer vision technology to enhance the production process of traditional Nepali Lokta paper, helping preserve cultural heritage while improving production efficiency.

Key Contributions:

  • Developed computer vision algorithms using OpenCV to detect defects, inconsistencies, and quality variations in Lokta paper
  • Created a custom dataset of Lokta paper samples with various defects to train the detection models
  • Implemented image processing techniques to identify texture inconsistencies, thickness variations, and impurities
  • Designed a user-friendly interface for operators to review flagged issues and maintain quality control records
  • Integrated the system with existing production workflows to minimize disruption

Outcomes:

  • Reduced manual inspection time by 70%, allowing artisans to focus on the craft aspects of production
  • Improved defect detection accuracy to 95%, exceeding human inspection rates
  • Decreased rejection rates by identifying and addressing quality issues earlier in the production process
  • Created a standardized quality assessment system for a traditionally subjective evaluation process

CoolBot vs Conventional Refrigeration Research

Sustainable Cold Storage Solutions

This project involved conducting a comprehensive comparative analysis between CoolBot technology and conventional refrigeration systems for cold storage applications, identifying significant advantages in energy efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and implementation considerations for different contexts.

Key Contributions:

  • Researched and analyzed technical specifications, energy consumption patterns, and operational costs of CoolBot and conventional refrigeration systems
  • Conducted field tests to measure and compare temperature stability, humidity control, and power consumption under various conditions
  • Developed a decision framework to help stakeholders choose appropriate cold storage technology based on their specific needs and constraints
  • Created detailed cost-benefit models accounting for initial investment, operational expenses, and maintenance requirements
  • Analyzed environmental impact factors including energy efficiency and refrigerant choices

Outcomes:

  • Identified that CoolBot systems offer 25-30% energy savings compared to conventional systems in specific applications
  • Demonstrated 40-60% lower initial installation costs for CoolBot systems while maintaining comparable performance in appropriate settings
  • Produced a comprehensive seminar paper and presentation documenting findings and implementation recommendations
  • Created decision-making tools adopted by local agricultural cooperatives for cold storage planning
